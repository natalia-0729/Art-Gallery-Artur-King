ARTUR KING
Painter

My name is Artem Papyan. I am a designer, artist, psychologist and diversified person. I paint my paintings under the pseudonym "Artur King". In my paintings I use different writing techniques. For me, paintings are not just drawings. The picture should catch the viewer, with its mystery and evoke emotion in a person.

Dolphins symbolize strength, freedom, joy, nobility, security, love, as well as connection with the sea. The dolphin lives in two elements: sea and air. It serves as a connecting link between the earthly and the heavenly and is the king of pisces, the savior of shipwrecked people, the guide of souls in the other world.
