Monotype | Abstract watercolor mystical painting "Homecoming"

Size:

20 W x 25 H x 0.1 D cm.

Category:

Abstract, Monotype

Materials:

Paper, Ink, Watercolor

Styles:

Abstract, Contemporary

Created:

2019

Buy

The picture shows the moment the wanderer returns home. In the foreground, we see the figure of a woman in a robe walking towards her old house, in front of which the same familiar tree grows.

On the right side, you can see a moored ship with already assembled sails, which brought our wanderer. It seems that birds are flying, but there is no that joyful feeling. Decrepit and forgotten old. The house is old and gray.

During the time the wanderer was gone, nothing changed, and even got worse. The return does not show the wanderer's joy or disappointment, but simply personifies the reality of being. Everything is as it is.
