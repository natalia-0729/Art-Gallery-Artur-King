The pug is one of those breeds that, thanks to its characteristic appearance, is easily recognized even by those who do not consider themselves an expert in dog breeding.

A pug cannot be called a guard dog, but it fits perfectly to the position of a wonderful friend. This is a dog that always causes joy and positive emotions. His slightly goofy facial expression and bulging eyes give him a memorable appearance.

While living in Turkey, I was inspired by the attitude of the Turkish people towards pets, stray dogs and cats. The Turks' love for animals impressed me. I have been to many countries and such a kind; loving relationship has not often met. I was inspired by this and I wanted to write a couple of works on the topic of dogs.

Stray dogs in Turkey are very kind and are drawn to people. They are always fed and stroked. They walk among people like our smaller brothers.

This picture can be a great gift or addition to your collection.
