Oil painting, abstract still life "Red roses and violin". This picture is a continuation of the story with the still life "Yellow roses and violin"

In the picture, I conveyed the moment at the end of the musician's concert. Red roses are a symbol of love and recognition. Our invisible hero has a muse, he reveals his creativity to the world. The world recognized him. In this picture, the violin is painted already with strings, which is the opposite of the picture with yellow roses. Glasses are a symbol of improved vision, and also indicate the continuation of the musician's career in the future.

This picture can be a great gift or addition to your collection.
