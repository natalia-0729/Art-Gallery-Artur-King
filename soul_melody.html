Melody of the Soul | Abstract figurative oil painting

Size:

63 W x 83 H x 2.5 D cm.

Category:

Abstract

Materials:

Oil paints, Canvas

Styles:

Abstract, Contemporary

Created:

2017

Abstract oil painting "melody of the soul". The painting depicts a naked girl playing the violin. The moment is captured when she finished her musical part and her soul is torn off the body. Fishing into the astral from the beauty of music. The whole world around is distorted and refracted. Three candles are burning near the girl. This is a symbol of the trinity, the elements of fire and the passing of time.

Our world consists of vibrations of various waves. Energy and sound. Light, color and sound have waves that affect a person. In this picture, I tried to convey not only the meaning of the waves in our world, both in the colors of the picture and in the sense of the plot, but also the beauty of female nature.
