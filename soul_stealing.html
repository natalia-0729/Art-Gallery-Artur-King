Monotype | Abstract watercolor mystical painting "Soul-Stealing"

Size:

28 W x 37 H x 0.1 D cm.

Category:

Abstract, Monotype

Materials:

Paper, Ink, Watercolor

Styles:

Abstract, Contemporary

Created:

2011

The painting depicts an abstract history of the relationship between a man and a woman. A man fights with snakes, and a woman steals his soul with light and refined movements. She didn't have much time left. This is a good image of a merged hourglass. It seems that time is running out.

Perhaps the lady is bewitching the man, trying to keep him near her, since she does not have much time left. Feeds on his life force.

In the distance we see a big city with skyscrapers and at the same time a path with leaving figures.

Each viewer of this work can interpret the story in their own way. It may be tired of big cities and losing its youth in the hustle and bustle of city life.
