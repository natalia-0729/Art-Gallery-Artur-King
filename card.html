ARTUR KING
Painter

My name is Artem Papyan. I am a designer, artist, psychologist and diversified person. I paint my paintings under the pseudonym "Arthur King". In my paintings I use different writing techniques. For me, paintings are not just drawings. The picture should catch the viewer, with its mystery and evoke emotion in a person.

The description of this picture was inspired by the Cane Corso dog breed. This wonderful breed conveys severity, a piercing look, but at the same time, devotion, courageous character and strength in these beautiful animals. In front of me, as an artist, an award was presented to the energy of a dog in front of my eyes.

The portrait is scary and kind. Let this dog belong to the owner of the protection of his home, inspired and give confidence thanks to the energy that I put into the description of this picture. Author of the painting: Arthur King
