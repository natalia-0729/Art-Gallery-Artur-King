Yellow roses and violin | Abstract oil painting still life

Size:

70 W x 50 H x 2.5 D cm.

Category:

Still-life

Materials:

Oil paints, Canvas

Styles:

Abstract, Contemporary

Created:

2022

Oil painting, abstract still life "yellow roses and violin".

In this picture, I wanted to convey the moment when the musician finished his concert. Yellow roses symbolize separation or the end of a relationship. In this case, it is the relationship between the musician and his performance. The candle is also a symbol of time. In the picture, she is depicted dying.

The musician finished playing his part, and maybe his musical career. This is shown by a violin that has no strings. They were taken off.

But not all is lost, hope remains. This is indicated by two buds of red roses that have not yet blossomed, but are not quite closed either. Also white sheets of paper under the violin. This is a symbol of the beginning.

Perhaps the musician and composer reached the crisis of his work and finished the last concert. But he hopes that music will overtake him and she will create new beautiful music that she will give to the world.
