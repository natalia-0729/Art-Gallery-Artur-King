ARTUR KING
Painter

My name is Artem Papyan. I am a designer, artist, psychologist and diversified person. I paint my paintings under the pseudonym "Artur King". In my paintings I use different writing techniques. For me, paintings are not just drawings. The picture should catch the viewer, with its mystery and evoke emotion in a person.

Butterflies are a symbol of the soul, immortality, rebirth and resurrection, the ability to transform. There is hope for a bright future with the appearance of butterflies. It seems that in a moment there will be a bright streak in life, and life will sparkle with bright colors.But before that, we need to pass the last obstacle that fate has presented to us. And the butterflies that embody your state of mind are making their way through thunder and inclement weather to fly away to a bright future.
