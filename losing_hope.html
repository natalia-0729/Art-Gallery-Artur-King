My name is Artem Papyan. I am a designer, artist, psychologist and diversified person. I paint my paintings under the pseudonym "Artur King". In my paintings I use different writing techniques. For me, paintings are not just drawings. The picture should catch the viewer, with its mystery and evoke emotion in a person.

Monotype | Abstract watercolor mystical painting "Losing hope"

28 W x 37 H x 0.1 D cm.

Sometimes life challenges us. Either we will survive and become stronger, or we will perish in the abyss of our fears, doubts and insecurities. At this moment, various demons of fear, despair and uncertainty take possession of a person. Hope for a brighter future is fading. This picture conveys this state and says that even in the most desperate state, there is always a ship of hope on the horizon that you can see.

The mystical picture in detail conveys the plot of one of the periods of my mother's life. Hard times in the 1990s The collapse of the USSR, poverty and complete despair.

This period forced my mother, a fragile woman, to move to another country (Turkey), where her career as an artist began.

After leaving her parents and separated from her child, Lydia sets off on a "ship of hope" to a new unknown life. She defeated her demons and made a decision that changed her life before and after.
