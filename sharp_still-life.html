Peonies, wine and fan | Abstract oil painting still life

Size:

70 W x 50 H x 2.5 D cm.

Category:

Still-life

Materials:

Oil paints, Canvas

Styles:

Abstract, Contemporary

Created:

2022

The oil painting "Sharp still life" depicts a bouquet of peonies in a vase, a bottle of wine and a fan.

The painting conveys a sense of sophistication and femininity. Due to light, pastel colors, this feeling is emphasized more strongly. But the style shows kinks, angularities and sharpness. That conveys the other side of female nature.
