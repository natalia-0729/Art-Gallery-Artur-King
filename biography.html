CONTACT ME

Hi! My name is Artem Papyan.

I am a designer, artist, psychologist and diversified person.

I am fond of esotericism, photography and do kung fu wing chun.

I paint my paintings under the pseudonym "Artur King".

In my paintings I use different writing techniques. Mystical tematics attract me the most. Through my paintings, I convey the energy of the relationship between good and evil.

Since in my understanding these are two components of one whole, like yin and yang. One is in balance with the other.

I also love animals and sometimes I paint parts of dogs or wild animals. In my work, I am constantly evolving and can change my style of writing and images in the paintings to more clearly convey the idea or what I want to say to the world through my paintings.

For me, paintings are not just drawings. The picture should catch the viewer, with its mystery and evoke emotion in a person.

My works can perfectly complement your collection of paintings or serve as a good gift.
